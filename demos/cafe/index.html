Our Story

A neighbourhood cafe, made for lingering.

Brew & Bloom started with one idea — a place where the coffee is taken seriously and the pace isn't. We roast in small batches, bake fresh every morning, and keep the good seats by the window. Whether it's your first meeting or your third refill, you're home here.
